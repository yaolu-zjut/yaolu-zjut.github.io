Biography

I’m Yao Lu. My advisors are Xiaoniu Yang and Qi Xuan. My research interest are interpretable machine learning and efficient learning. I was graduated from Zhejiang University of Technology with a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering and its automation on June, 2021. I am always open for academic collaboration. You can contact me through email yaolu.zjut@gmail.com.
